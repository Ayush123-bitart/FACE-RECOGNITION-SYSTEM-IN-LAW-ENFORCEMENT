About This Project

This project Face Recognition in Law Enforcement is developed as part of our MCA course. It demonstrates how face recognition can help in investigations by providing live alerts, match statistics, and camera feed monitoring.

The dashboard is designed to simulate real-time monitoring where law enforcement agencies can track matches, unmatched faces, and case statistics in a user-friendly way.
